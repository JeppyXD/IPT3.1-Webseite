Forza Horizon 5 Series 6 Update

Horizon Customs

Horizon Customs. The focus of the sixth Forza Horizon 5 series is 'Horizon Customs', which celebrates all the various ways players can contribute to FH5 by creating their own creations, including songs and vehicle designs, EventLab Blueprints and photographers. Some of the changes that have come with this playlist festival include:

  • New collectible paint barrels and miniature t-rex dinosaurs scattered all over the world

  • Two EventLab Events featured every week

  • One Seasonal Championship featuring a custom photo taken by a community member

  • Seasonal PR Stunts will now provide Super Wheelspins instead of regular Wheelspins

  • Tons of new cosmetics and cars
